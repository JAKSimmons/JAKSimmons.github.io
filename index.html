Andrew Simmons

I am a Junior at Bob Jones University pursuing a degree in Flute Performance. I work for Piano Tech as well as the Percussion Moving Crew at the university. I also work at Musical Innovations in Mauldin as well as teach flute lessons.

I enjoy performing with the University's Wind Band, Flute Choir, and Symphony Orchestra. I have performed with these ensambles for the past three years. Before I came to Bob Jones, I performed with the Greenville County Youth Orchestra's Young Artist Orchestra for four years. I also enjoy playing in my church orchestra.
